Professional Bio

Ben helps keep the world's most important supply chains operational at Palantir. His expertise in supply chain optimization, granular COGS, and connected revenue management is drawn from over five years of leading engagements with industry leaders in aviation, defense, CPG, mining/metals, manufacturing, and travel/leisure.

Prior to joining Palantir, Ben led an award-winning redesign of SimpliSafeâ€™s home security system, founded a hardware startup making head impact sensors for athletes (earning recognition on the Forbes 30 Under 30 list in Healthcare), and completed the global management trainee program at AB InBev. He studied mechanical engineering and architecture at MIT after graduating high school in three years.

Personal Bio

Born and raised in central Maryland, Ben now lives in New Hampshire with his wife and daughter. In his free time, he enjoys working with his hands and doing anything that gets him outside. Lately, surveying, trail cutting, and barn/cabin construction on his 50 acre property checks both boxes (and takes up plenty of time).

When he's not swinging a hammer, Ben and his wife enjoy "highpointing", having climbed {{ site.categories.highpoints | size }} state highpoints as part of their effort to summit the highest natural point in each of the 50 states.
