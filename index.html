Ben Harvatine

MIT Engineer.
Founder & CEO at Jolt.
Forbes 30 Under 30.

Ben is a thinker/tinkerer with an entrepreneurial streak. Prior to founding Jolt, he co-founded Birddog Innovations and was a Global Management Trainee at Anheuser-Busch InBev. Ben is a MassChallenge, Techstars, and MIT alum.

He has been a lifelong athlete, playing football, soccer, baseball, swimming, and wrestling growing up. Through the years, Ben wracked up a number of concussions, including a rather debilitating one at wrestling practice during his junior year of college. Since then he has been focused on improving concussion education and sports safety technology, and has been recognized on the 2016 Forbes 30 Under 30 list in Healthcare.

Ben holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering, with a minor in Architecture, from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. In his free time, he enjoys doing anything that gets him outside -- sailing, tennis, fishing, hunting, and mountaineering -- and working with his hands -- tinkering with his 3D printer, wrenching on his truck, and building furniture.
